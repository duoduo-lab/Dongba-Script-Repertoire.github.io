Plate of Radicals in Li et al 1972

This page provides an overview of Dongba radicals based on Li Lincan et al. (1972). The English terms of each category are quoted from Li et al. (2001: 419-421), translated by Tseng Chao-yueh. Additional glyphs incorporated to the current font are available as an extension list .
